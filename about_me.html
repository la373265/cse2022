About Me

My name is Lorena Alvarez and I'm a junior at Santa Ana High School. Computer Science Essentials is the first Project Lead the Way (PLTW) course I've enrolled in. Despite it being a course designed for 3rd year PLTW students, my persistence and skills have helped me keep up with the class. Other STEM courses I'm enrolled in are AP Calculus AB and AP Environmental Science. Although I've always enjoyed my STEM courses at school, Computer Science is the field I want to pursue in the future as a Software Engineer. As for my hobbies, during my free time I like to read, spend time with friends, and go on bike rides.

Why take Computer Science Essentials?
I decided to enroll in Computer Science Essentials because the summer before my junior year, I became interested in coding. CSE seemed like a great opportunity to learn more about coding and how those skills can translate in a work environment. I will continue on the PLTW pathway here at Santa Ana High School because it will give me a headstart to what I will learn in college.
